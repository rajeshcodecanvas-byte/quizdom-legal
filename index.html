Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2024

This privacy policy applies to the Q-Master mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). The App is provided by CodeCanvas as a Free service and is intended for use "AS IS".

The App's branding identity and motto is "Q-Master: Claim Your Intellectual Throne".

1. Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information (such as your name, email address, or device identifiers). All game progress, high scores, and quiz results are stored locally on your device using a Room database. No user data is ever uploaded to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you (such as Advertising IDs). We recommend reading the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Advertising)

3. External Resources (Flag Assets)

To provide an immersive educational experience, the App dynamically fetches national flag images from FlagCDN. While the App is optimized for offline play, an internet connection is used to load these visual assets. These requests are standard HTTPS requests and do not transmit any personal user identifiers.

4. Children’s Privacy

Q-Master does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since all data is stored locally, any information related to gameplay remains under the control of the device owner.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All user progress (high scores and historical data) is stored locally. This data is retained until the App is uninstalled or you use the "Reset Realm Progress" feature within the Settings menu to wipe your local database.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, your information is as secure as your physical device. We recommend using biometric or PIN security on your device to protect your local data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically. Any updates will be reflected here immediately upon publication.
