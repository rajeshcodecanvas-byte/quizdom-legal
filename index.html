Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2024

This privacy policy applies to the QuizDom mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). The App is provided by CodeCanvas as a Free service and is intended for use "AS IS".

The App's branding and motto is "QuizDom: Master the Trivia Realm".

1. Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. All game progress, high scores, and quiz data are stored locally on your device using a Room/SQLite database. No user data is ever uploaded to our own servers.

2. Third-Party Services & External Resources

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Links to the privacy policies of third-party service providers used by the App:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Advertising)
  • FlagCDN (External Flag Assets)

3. Network Usage (Flag Images)

To provide a high-quality educational experience, the App fetches national flag images from FlagCDN. While the App is designed for offline use, an internet connection is required to initially load these visual assets. These requests do not transmit any personal user identifiers.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Data Retention

All data (scores, history) is stored locally on your device. This data is retained until the App is uninstalled or you clear the App's storage via your device settings.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since no personal data is collected, your information is as secure as your physical device. We recommend using device-level security (PIN/Biometrics) to protect your data.
