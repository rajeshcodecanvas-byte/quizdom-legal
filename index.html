Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

QuizDom: Master the Trivia Realm is built as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by CodeCanvas at no cost and is intended for use as is.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. All game progress, high scores, and quiz data are stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. No user data is ever uploaded to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services & External Resources

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Additionally, the app fetches visual resources (National Flags) from external providers to enhance the learning experience.

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Advertising)
  • FlagCDN (External Flag Assets)

3. Network Usage

While QuizDom is primarily an offline experience, a network connection is used to fetch high-quality flag images for the "Country Knowledge" section. These requests do not contain any personal identifiers.

4. Security

We value your trust. Since no personal data is collected or uploaded, your information is as secure as your physical device. We recommend keeping your device OS updated for the best security.
