Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2024

This privacy policy applies to the QuizDom mobile application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). The App is provided by CodeCanvas as a Free service and is intended for use "AS IS".

The App's branding identity and motto is "QuizDom: Master the Trivia Realm".

1. Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number). All game progress, high scores, and quiz data are stored locally on your device using a secure database. No user data is ever uploaded to our own servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you (such as Advertising IDs). We recommend reading the privacy policies of these providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (Advertising)

3. External Resources (Flag Assets)

To provide a high-quality educational experience, the App fetches national flag images from FlagCDN. While the App is designed for offline play, an internet connection is used to load these visual assets. These requests are standard HTTP requests and do not contain any personal user identifiers.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our local records.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All user data (scores and history) is stored locally on your device. This data is retained until the App is uninstalled or you manually clear the App's storage in your device settings.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since no personal data is collected or uploaded, your information is as secure as your physical device. We recommend using device-level security (PIN/Biometrics) and keeping your Android OS updated.
